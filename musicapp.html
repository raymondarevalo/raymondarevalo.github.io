Spotify / Apple Music Redesign
This project is a prototype of a mobile music streaming application. By taking a simple approach to redesigning the information architecture of a music application, it provides a intuitive way for users to construct, manage, and search for music and playlists.

This project was designed along with 4 other students in COGS 187a, a UX course at UC San Diego. Throughout the 4 week process, my team and I conducted user interviews, tested workflows and did a competitive analysis of information architecture of major music applications, and did a prototype redesign of Spotify and Apple Music.

Water Tracker
User centered prototype of a water usage tracking system for college dormitories. Displays appartment's and building's water usage against suggested amounts. Aimed at changing students behavior towards water conservation.

Identified the design problem and design solution through the use of user research, prototyping, and wireframing.
Part 1 Problem / Challenge / Opportunity
Conceptual dimensions, physical dimensions, social dimensions, research methods
Part 2 Target Audience
Demographics, Pychographics, Relevance
Part 3 Design Solution
Physical perceivable features, overarching system, form and funciton, User Benefits

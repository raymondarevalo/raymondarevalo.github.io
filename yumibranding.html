Outcome
Working on implementing and improving Yumi's branding was my favorite part of the job. Although most of my ideas never saw a light of day, working on branding gave me the opportunity to explore my creative side. I dove deeper into a love for typography and into areas I was not comfortable in. Being adaptive was very important as I needed to learn how to work with fun and whimsical visual elements.

Most importantly, I learned how important telling stories, communication, trust, and perception was in branding.

Outcome
From finding new ways to integrate Yumi's brand with our Chef's Series collaborators, to making Yumi stand out from its competitors with hip and trendy merchandise, to making the brand cohesive by integrating online design elements into physical collateral, working on implementing and improving Yumi's branding was my favorite part of the job.

Although most of my ideas never saw a light of day, working on branding gave me the opportunity to explore my creative side. I dove deeper into a love for typography and into areas I was not comfortable in. Being adaptive was very important as I needed to learn how to work with fun and whimsical visual elements.

Most importantly, I learned how important telling stories, communication, trust, and perception was in branding.
